Who It's For

Ambient soundscapes aren't just background noise—they're tools for wellbeing.

Built from Personal Need

Sonorium was created by someone who is AuDHD (autism + ADHD) and needs ambient sound to function. Background audio quiets the part of the brain that's really good at distracting everything else.

  • ADHD & Focus: White noise and nature sounds help filter distractions
  • Misophonia: Ambient masking covers trigger sounds
  • Sensory Processing: Predictable, soothing auditory input
  • Anxiety & Stress: Nature sounds promote relaxation
  • Sleep: Mask disruptive noises for better rest
  • Work & Study: The "coffee shop effect" boosts creativity
