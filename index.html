Everything You Need

Powerful features to create the perfect ambient atmosphere in every room.

Multi-Channel Audio

Run up to 10 independent audio channels simultaneously. Each channel can play a different theme to different speakers.

Theme-Based Mixing

Organize audio into themes that automatically blend together. Thunder, rain, wind—they all mix seamlessly in real-time.

Track Mixer

Fine-tune individual tracks with volume, presence, and playback controls. Create the exact atmosphere you want.

Presets

Save your favorite track configurations as presets. Switch between "morning focus" and "deep sleep" modes instantly.

Export & Import

Package themes as zip files to share with others. Import themes from the community with a single click.

Plugin System

Extend functionality with plugins. Import soundscapes from ambient-mixer.com or build your own integrations.

Who It's For

Ambient soundscapes aren't just background noise—they're tools for wellbeing.

Built from Personal Need

Sonorium was created by someone who is AuDHD (autism + ADHD) and needs ambient sound to function. Background audio quiets the part of the brain that's really good at distracting everything else.

  • ADHD & Focus: White noise and nature sounds help filter distracting sounds and improve concentration
  • Misophonia: Ambient masking helps cover trigger sounds and reduce emotional responses
  • Sensory Processing: Controlled ambient environments provide predictable, soothing auditory input
  • Anxiety & Stress: Nature sounds activate the parasympathetic nervous system, promoting relaxation
  • Sleep: Consistent ambient sound masks disruptive noises for better rest
  • Work & Study: The "coffee shop effect"—moderate ambient noise boosts creative thinking
